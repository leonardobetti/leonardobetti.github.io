TBD - Websites, Presentations and Branding


Below are a series of graphic layouts which demonstrate the brand identity of The Business Debate as well as the services it provides to its wide variety of clients.

The Business Debate is an established international production company specialising in solution based journalism. The company produce B2B video content for global brands and SMEs that are disseminated worldwide through a network of prestigious media platforms such as Bloomberg, The Telegraph and CNN. The company launch these videos using bespoke news hubs. Each hub is supported by an extensive marketing and social media activation plan which enhances the reach and organic traffic of these hubs. The Business Debate amplifies its clients content to create maximum exposure across each digital media platform by utilising press releases, paid advertising, native marketing and social media.

Goals

Layout consistency

One of my key objectives was to design a new single source of truth for the company’s brand identity. This was to create consistency in the graphical layout of websites, pitch decks, presentations and marketing materials. I created new style guides, brand assets and a comprehensive pattern library. This refresh of the brand identity enabled the sales and marketing teams to win a number of successful pitches with global brands including Microsoft and Nestlé.

Website refresh

TBD use different campaigns websites to promote content to clients and generate leads. My task was to create entirely new websites for each campaign and ensure they were updated in real time. The objective was to enable visitors to navigate the websites quickly and efficiently using multiple platforms including mobile and tablet. This required extensive knowledge of UX and UI principles as well as a strong creative vision.
