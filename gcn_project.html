GCN Cleaning Services receiving more leads


The project aimed to create a powerful tool that brought leads, during their rebrand and web design update

GCN Cleaning Services is a small and reputable cleaning company that operates in Bristol UK, specialises in working with all type of businesses and residential properties. The company proposal offers cleaning services around Bristol, also presents as a reliable and competitive solution to offices and houses.

Goals

Website update

I’ve made an upgrade on GCN Cleaning Services brand guidelines, affecting their digital status. My main goal was to build, test and validate the new website.

Start Social Media plan

Since I joined the project, the presence on Social Media didn’t exist, which means that nobody recognises GCN Cleaning Services as a modern company. My task was to build the Facebook fanpage with the stakeholders using the website Figma to create and approve the posts contents.

Brand alignment

By putting brand values at the heart of your customer experience, I create interfaces that are meaningful to the client. I’ve made a research to gather what promises GCN Cleaning Services customers project about the company service.


Research Insights

Their previous Marketing plan based in an amateur approach, giving away leaflets and business cards. Before I started, I’ve decide to interview five previous customers to collect quality data about the GCN Cleaning Services brand attributes. With the results, it will be suitable to create a brand statement that guides the visual identity. We collect the data using interview techniques to let the user lead a particular point of interest.

Information Architecture / Wireframes

To create information architecture I adopted to the method of card-sorting. The goal was to provide inputs from a user’s perspective whereas I can bring in the interaction design principles. Basically I organize topics into categories that make sense to GCN Cleaning Services customers and they may also help label these groups. After that, I’ve created the sitemap in 5 internal pages: Home, About Us, Services, Office Cleaning, Contact Us.

Test, Improve & Update

After launching the website also the facebook page I booked a week of user-testing with me and the company stakeholders. This team regularly engaged with the users to gather their insights to make improvements. As a example, we received complaints about the number of fields to fill the Contact Form. One of the challenges that we were facing regards GCN Cleaning Service is to produce relevant content to post on Social Media, so we can attract new leads to interact with.
